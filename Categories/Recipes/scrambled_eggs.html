Scrammbled Eggs

Description

Four scrambled eggs cooked well done to give you that morning "pick-me up!"" Great source of protein and can be served with some hot sauce for spicy deliciousness!

Ingredients

  • 4 eggs
  • 1/2 tsp butter
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • 1/4 tsp parsley flakes
  • 1 tbsp tapatio hot sauce (optional for extra flavoring)

Servings

  • 5 minute prep time
  • 10 minute cook time
  • Serves 2

How to Prepare

  1. Crack four eggs into a bowl and mix with a fork thoroughly until mixture is uniform
  2. Heat a medium pan over low-medium heat and add butter to pan to coat it
  3. Pour in egg mixture to pan and let it cook. As the eggs are coocking, use a spatula to move the cooked parts of the eggs to the center of the pan and tilt the pan so that the runny parts are at the edge of the pan. Repeat the process until the majority of the eggs are cooked
  4. Mash eggs with a spatula to scramble them then carefully pour the scrambled eggs on a plate
  5. Season eggs with salt, pepper, parsley flakes, and optionally, hot sauce then enjoy!
